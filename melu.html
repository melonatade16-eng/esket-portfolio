About Ambo University

Ambo University is a public national university located in Ambo, Oromia Region, Ethiopia, about 114 km west of Addis Ababa. It is recognized by the Ministry of Education and offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs. It also has multiple campuses, including the main campus in Ambo, as well as Waliso, Awaro, and Guder campuses.
